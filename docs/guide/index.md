Welcome to the Yukinu User Guide. This section provides a practical, role-based overview of how to use the platform's features.

Whether you are a customer, a vendor, or an administrator, these guides will walk you through the key functionalities available to you.

## Guides by Role

- **[2.1. Customer Guide](./customer.md)**: Learn how to browse and search for products, manage your account, use the shopping cart and wishlist, and track your orders.

- **[2.2. Vendor Guide](./vendor.md)**: Find out how to manage your store, list and update products, process customer orders, and manage your staff.

- **[2.3. Administrator Guide](./admin.md)**: Understand how to oversee the marketplace, manage users and vendors, moderate content, and configure system-wide settings.
