## Account Management

- **Registration**: You can create a new account using your email and a password.
- **Login**: Securely log in to your account to access your personal information and order history.
- **Profile Updates**: You can update your personal details, such as your name and contact information, from your account dashboard.
- **Address Book**: Manage multiple shipping addresses for a faster checkout experience. You can add, edit, and delete addresses as needed.

## Shopping

- **Browsing Products**: Explore products by category or view all available items in the main product listing.
- **Advanced Search**: Use the search bar to find specific products. You can filter results by category, price range, and other attributes.
- **Product Details**: Click on any product to view its detailed description, specifications, images, and vendor information.
- **Wishlist**: Not ready to buy? Add products to your personal wishlist to save them for later. You can view your wishlist at any time from your account page.

## Purchasing

- **Shopping Cart**: Add items to your shopping cart from one or more vendors. You can adjust quantities or remove items before proceeding to checkout.
- **Unified Checkout**: The platform supports a unified checkout process, allowing you to pay for items from multiple vendors in a single transaction.
- **Order Placement**: Follow the on-screen instructions to select your shipping address, choose a payment method, and confirm your order.

## After Purchase

- **Order History**: All your past orders are listed in your account dashboard.
- **Order Tracking**: View the current status of your orders, from "Processing" to "Shipped" and "Delivered."
- **Cancellations & Reviews**: Depending on the vendor's policy and the order status, you may be able to request a cancellation or leave a review for a completed purchase.
