---
title: 4. Use Cases
description: This document outlines the various use cases and services available in the application. These services represent the core business logic and features of the platform.
---

## 4.01. [Authentication](./auth.md)

Manages user authentication, including login, logout, registration, and session management.

## 4.02. [User Management](./user.md)

Manages user accounts, including profiles, settings, and roles.

## 4.03. [Security](./security.md)

Provides security-related services, such as managing API keys and other security measures.

## 4.04. [Address Management](./address.md)

Handles all operations related to user addresses, including creating, updating, deleting, and retrieving address information.

## 4.05. [Vendor Management](./vendor.md)

Manages vendor information and their relationship with the platform.

## 4.06. [Vendor Staff Management](./vendor-staff.md)

Manages staff accounts associated with a vendor.

## 4.07. [Category Management](./category.md)

Manages product categories, including creating, updating, and deleting categories.

## 4.08. [Product Management](./product.md)

Handles all aspects of products, including their creation, updates, and deletion.

## 4.09. [Product Variant Management](./product-variant.md)

Manages different variants of a product, such as size, color, and other attributes.

## 4.10. [Wishlist](./wishlist.md)

Manages user wishlists, allowing them to save products for later.

## 4.11. [Shopping Cart](./shopping-cart.md)

Handles all shopping cart operations, such as adding, removing, and updating items in the cart.

## 4.12. [Order Management](./order.md)

Manages customer orders, including order creation, status updates, and tracking.

## 4.13. [Banner Management](./banner.md)

Manages promotional banners that are displayed in the application.

## 4.14. [Support Tickets](./ticket.md)

Manages customer support tickets for handling inquiries and issues.
